                <h3>Days 0–30: Understand, Instrument, Prioritize</h3>

                <p><strong>Goal:</strong> Get fluent in the funnel and choose the

                  first 3–5 high-leverage experiments.</p>

                <ul>

                  <li>

                    Map the self-serve funnel end-to-end: traffic sources &rarr;

                    signup &rarr; first project/deploy &rarr; activation &rarr;

                    upgrade &rarr; expansion.

                  </li>

                  <li>

                    Audit performance for SEO, GEO, paid, and on-site conversion

                    (pricing, docs entry pages, templates).

                  </li>

                  <li>

                    Review existing experiments and analytics with Growth Product

                    and Data; align on a clear north star for self-serve.

                  </li>

                  <li>

                    Segment users into practical cohorts (solo devs, agencies,

                    startups, enterprise evaluators).

                  </li>

                  <li>

                    Produce a simple, shared dashboard for self-serve funnel

                    health and experiment impact.

                  </li>

                </ul>

              </div>

              <div>

                <h3>Days 31–60: Ship High-Impact Experiments</h3>

                <p><strong>Goal:</strong> Launch and learn from 3–5 meaningful

                  tests across acquisition, activation, and monetization.</p>

                <ul>

                  <li>

                    Acquisition: sharpen high-intent SEO and GEO, refine paid

                    search/social around ICPs, and test new landers and offers.

                  </li>

                  <li>

                    Activation: experiment with onboarding flows, starter

                    templates, and nudges to shorten time-to-first-deploy.

                  </li>

                  <li>

                    Monetization: test messaging and CTAs on pricing pages and

                    in-app upgrade prompts aligned to usage thresholds.

                  </li>

                  <li>

                    Create a light-weight experiment doc format and a weekly

                    growth standup to keep the loop tight.

                  </li>

                </ul>

              </div>

            </div>


            <h3>Days 61–90: Scale What Works & Systematize Growth</h3>

            <p><strong>Goal:</strong> Turn early wins into durable, repeatable

              programs.</p>

            <ul>

              <li>

                Scale the best-performing campaigns and experiences while

                maintaining CAC/payback guardrails.

              </li>

              <li>

                Build a rolling experimentation roadmap with Growth Product and

                Demand Gen so the engine keeps running after month 3.

              </li>

              <li>

                Tighten reporting for leadership: a clear narrative around

                traffic, activation, conversion, and revenue from self-serve.

              </li>

              <li>

                Document learnings so future teammates can build on a strong

                foundation rather than reinventing tests.

              </li>

            </ul>
